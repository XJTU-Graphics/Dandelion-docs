18/*!
19 * \ingroup ui
20 * \~english
21 * \brief The controller manages all UI components and processes input to rotate, zoom or
22 * translate the scene.
23 *
24 * The life cycle of the controller is as long as the whole GUI, thus this is a singleton class,
25 * the only instance can be accessed through the static method `controller()`. The accessing
26 * operation is thread safe while modification is not. The controller creates and holds
27 * all resource related with GUI, including
28 *
29 * - GUI elements (widgets) such as the menubar and toolbar.
30 * - The 3D scene consists of objects, camera, lights and axes.
31 *
32 * The GUI widgets hold neither the scene nor its reference. Instead, the controller pass
33 * a reference to the scene to them by their `render` method, allowing them to access and
34 * modify the scene. Doing so does not have advantage against having widgets hold a reference,
35 * we just design it as Scotty3D, which may benefit global undo and redo operations in the
36 * future.
37 *
38 * \~chinese
39 * \brief 控制器管理所有的界面组件，并处理和预览视角操作（例如旋转、缩放或者平移）相关的输入。
40 *
41 * 控制器的生命周期就是整个程序 GUI 的生命周期，因此这是一个单例的类。其唯一一个实例可以通过
42 * `controller()` 这个静态方法访问，访问操作是线程安全的（但访问后的修改不是）。
43 * 控制器创建并持有所有与图形界面相关的资源：
44 *
45 * - 界面元素（菜单栏和工具栏）
46 * - 场景（包含所有物体、坐标轴等）
47 *
48 * 界面元素本身不存储场景，也不保存指向场景的引用等。控制器在每一帧将场景的引用传递给
49 * `Toolbar::render` 或 `Menubar::render` 函数，从而允许这些界面元素访问场景。
50 * 与 `Menubar` 或 `Toolbar` 保存引用相比，这种设计当前并没有额外有点，它只是参考 Scotty3D
51 * 思路的结果，将来有可能为引入全局撤销 (Undo) 和重做 (Redo) 操作提供方便。
52 */
